Split

Version 1.1

Description

Split breaks a file into a specified number of equal pieces. These can be later reassembled into the original file.

Tutorial

To Split
1. Click on split.
2. Click on source.
3. Choose source file.
4. Click on destination.
5. Choose destination folder.
6. Choose number of pieces. Click on Split.
7. Wait for split.
8. Files have been created.
 
  

To Join
1. Click on join.
2. Click on source.
3. Click on first file.
4. Hold shift, click on last file.
5. Click on destination.
6. Choose destination folder.
7. Click on join.
8. Wait for join.
9. Original file has been restored.
